Research Studies

Research studies are designed to deeply understand your audience, uncover preferences, and identify behaviors that inform decisions. They provide the foundation for strategy and innovation.

Value: Acting on these studies allows organizations to target initiatives effectively and make informed decisions with confidence.

  • Customer Segmentation
  • Concept Testing
  • Customer Satisfaction
  • Usage and Attitudes
  • Brand Health
  • Donor Trust, Loyalty, and Retention Analysis

Tracking Studies

Tracking studies provide systematic, ongoing evaluation of key metrics. They offer a longitudinal perspective on trends and audience behavior, ensuring strategies remain aligned with evolving conditions.

Value: Organizations can adapt quickly, optimizing performance and maintaining strategic direction.

  • Donor Retention / Churn Rates
  • Customer Satisfaction Scores
  • Market Share
  • Net Promoter Score (NPS)
  • Engagement Rate
  • Donation Behavior
  • Awareness and Perception
  • Messaging Effectiveness
  • Program Effectiveness
  • Partnership Opportunities
